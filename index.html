Restoration of mechanical watches

Keep your watch running

At the heart of our watch restoration service is a team of dedicated horology specialists, each with years of experience working on some of the world's most intricate timepieces. Our experts are not just technicians—they are artisans who understand the soul of a mechanical watch. With backgrounds in traditional watchmaking and modern restoration, our team is equipped to breathe new life into vintage, heirloom, and luxury mechanical watches of all kinds.
We believe every watch has a story worth preserving. That's why our restoration process is rooted in meticulous attention to detail, historical accuracy, and a deep respect for the original craftsmanship. From delicate hand-polishing of components to reassembling complex movements, we ensure that every restored timepiece looks and functions as its maker intended—if not better. Our goal is to return your watch in pristine condition, with its character intact and its legacy extended.
Combining the finesse of traditional craftsmanship with the precision of modern technology, we utilize state-of-the-art equipment throughout our restoration process. Our workshop features advanced diagnostic tools, ultrasonic cleaning systems, and precision lathes that allow for unmatched accuracy in both assessment and repair. This seamless integration of heritage techniques and cutting-edge technology ensures your mechanical watch receives the highest standard of care.
